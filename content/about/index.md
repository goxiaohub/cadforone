**CAD For One** provides free CAD/CAM/CNC tutorials for mechanical engineers, CNC machinists, and mold makers.

Our tutorials focus on practical, step-by-step instructions with detailed screenshots to help you master:

- **UG NX** — Parametric modeling, sheet metal, mold design
- **FreeCAD** — Open-source CAD workflows
- **CAM** — Toolpath strategies and CNC programming
